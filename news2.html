Quebec reports 74 new COVID-19 cases, one death

Quebec public health authorities announced Sunday that 61,673 people have tested positive for the virus in the province, and 5,740 people have died due to the disease since the start of the pandemic.

Future priests move from Montreal's Grand Seminaire, ending its 160-year legacy

After decades of dwindling interest in the priesthood, the priests-in-training are leaving the historic halls of Montreal's Grand Seminaire -- which has a legacy dating back nearly to the city's founding -- and moving to smaller quarters across town.

Longshoremen return to work at the Port of Montreal as negotiations continue

The gradual return to work of the 1,125 longshoremen at the Port of Montreal began at 7 a.m. Sunday morning, as economic activities resumed at the Port of Montreal, where two labour disputes had broken out.
