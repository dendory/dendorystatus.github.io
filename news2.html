Quebec reports no new COVID-19 deaths, 145 new cases

There are 5,667 people who have died of COVID-19 in Quebec, health authorities announced Monday, as confirmed cases in the province reached 58,728.

Montreal adds 180 new COVID-19 cases since Friday, no new deaths

Montreal public health authorities are reporting 180 new COVID-19 cases since Friday for a total of 28,520 cases. There have been no new deaths since Friday, and so the total remains at 3,434.
