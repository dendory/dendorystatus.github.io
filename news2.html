A 19-year-old has died of COVID-19 in Quebec: health authorities

Quebec has seen very few deaths of young people during the COVID-19 pandemic, but on Thursday the province's health department confirmed that a 19-year-old has died from it.

Quebec reports 93 more positive COVID-19 cases, big drop in hospitalizations

Quebec public health authorities announced Friday that 61,495 people in the province have now tested positive for COVID-19 and 5,733 people have died due to the disease since the start of the pandemic.

Quebec announces $375M to keep post-secondary students from dropping out

With a goal of keeping students from dropping out, the Quebec government announced Friday an investment of $375 million in financial funding for post-secondary education.
