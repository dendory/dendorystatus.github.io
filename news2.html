Quebec COVID-19 positive cases reach 60,000; two new deaths

Quebec public health authorities announced Wednesday that 60,000 people have now tested positive for COVID-19 in the province, and 5,687 have died due to the virus.

Just over two per cent of Quebec's adult population contracted COVID-19: study

A serological study by Hema-Quebec and the province's public health institute has revealed that an estimated 2.23 per cent of Quebec's adult population has contracted COVID-19 since the start of the pandemic.

Quebec researchers achieve possible breakthrough in obesity and type 2 diabetes treatment

A team of researchers from Sherbrooke is reporting that they have achieved a scientific breakthrough that could improve the treatment of type 2 diabetes and obesity and prevent complications from the diseases.
