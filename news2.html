Quebec announces 141 new COVID-19 cases, three more deaths

Quebec public health officials announced Sunday that a total of 59,599 people have tested positive for COVID-19 since the start of the pandemic and 5,681 have died due to the novel coronavirus.

Public gatherings in Quebec can increase to 250 people as of Monday

The maximum size of a public gathering in Quebec is about to get a lot larger. As of Aug. 3, as many as 250 people can gather at an indoor or outdoor event, a significant increase from the 50 people currently authorized to do so.

Canada goose found with explosive duct taped to its body

A Long Island, N.Y. man has saved a Canada goose after finding the bird with an explosive device duct taped to its chest.
