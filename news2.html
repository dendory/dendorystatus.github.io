A new Federal Court ruling sends a clear message: the Roxham Road “irregular” border crossing shouldn’t exist. The court found that asylum seekers should be able to make claims at official land crossings instead.

Public gatherings in Quebec can increase to 250 people as of Aug. 3

The maximum size of a public gathering in Quebec is about to get a lot larger. As of Aug. 3, as many as 250 people can gather at an indoor or outdoor event, a significant increase from the 50 people currently authorized to do so.
