Quebec back-to-school plan requiring in-person attendance faces legal challenge

A lawyer representing a group of Quebec parents says he's moving forward with a legal challenge aimed at forcing the province to offer remote learning for families who don't want their children returning to classrooms this fall.

Quebec announced only 55 new COVID-19 cases on Monday, the fourth consecutive day with fewer than 100 new cases. Public health officials reported one additional death that had occurred between Aug. 10 and 15, but no new deaths in the past 24 hours.
