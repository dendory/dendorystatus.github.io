Masks now mandatory in all indoor public places across Quebec

Starting today, Quebec becomes the first province in Canada to require mask-wearing in all indoor public places.

A Montreal Children's Hospital campaign hopes to purchase 100,000 reusable masks

The Montreal Children's Hospital is stepping up its efforts to get everyone to wear a mask and has launched a campaign to buy 100,000 reusable face masks.

Bloc to back Liberals' bill extending wage subsidy, sending money to disabled

The Bloc Quebecois says it'll support a bill to extend the federal emergency wage subsidy and make payments to people with disabilities to help with COVID-19 costs when the House of Commons meets on Monday.
