Quebec reports no new COVID-19 deaths, 176 more cases

There are 5,670 people who have died of COVID-19 in Quebec, health authorities announced Wedneday, as confirmed cases in the province reached 59,073.

Day camp on Montreal's South Shore has 27 COVID-19 cases, not including secondary infections

Twenty-seven kids and staff members tied to a day camp in Boucherville have been infected with COVID-19, health authorities say—and that’s not counting secondary infections tied to the outbreak.

Environment Canada said the severe thunderstorm watch is in effect for the Montreal Island, Laval, Chateauguay-La Prairie and Longueuil-Varennes areas.
